# Maneuver Knowledge Base

## Company

Maneuver is a product design and development studio. We help founders and teams turn ideas into products people love — from early strategy through launch and growth.

## Services

### Product Strategy
We help you clarify your vision, validate assumptions, and define a roadmap that balances user needs with business goals. Ideal when you have an idea but need direction before building.

### UX/UI Design
End-to-end design — research, wireframes, high-fidelity UI, and design systems. We craft interfaces that feel intuitive and look polished.

### MVP Development
We build your minimum viable product fast — typically 6 to 8 weeks — using modern stacks so you can test with real users and iterate quickly.

### Growth Consulting
Post-launch support: analytics setup, conversion optimization, onboarding flows, and growth experiments to help you scale what works.

## Process

Our five-step process: **Discover → Define → Design → Develop → Deploy**

1. **Discover** — Understand your users, market, and constraints through research and workshops.
2. **Define** — Align on scope, success metrics, and a clear product definition.
3. **Design** — Create UX flows and visual design that solve the right problems.
4. **Develop** — Build the product with quality engineering and regular check-ins.
5. **Deploy** — Launch, measure, and hand off with documentation and support.
